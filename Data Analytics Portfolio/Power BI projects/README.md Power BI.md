### Power BI Supermarket Sales Dashboard



#### Project Overview



This project analyzes supermarket sales data using Power BI to identify sales trends, customer purchasing patterns, and product performance. The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics and helps support data-driven decision-making.



Dataset



The dataset used for this project is the Supermarket Sales Dataset obtained from Kaggle. It contains transaction-level information including product lines, sales revenue, customer types, cities, payment methods, ratings, and dates.



##### Business Questions



This dashboard was created to answer the following questions:



* What is the total revenue generated?
* Which product lines generate the highest sales?
* Which cities contribute the most revenue?
* How do sales change over time?
* What are the average customer ratings across product categories?

Dashboard Features



###### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)



* Total Revenue
* Total Profit (Gross Income)
* Total Products Sold



##### Visualizations



* Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
* Sales by Product Line (Bar Chart)
* Sales by City (Pie Chart)
* Average Rating by Product Line (Column Chart)



###### Interactive Features



* City Filter/Slicer
* Dynamic dashboard visuals based on selected filters



###### Tools Used



* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* Data Visualization Techniques



##### Key Insights



1. Certain product lines generated significantly higher revenue than others.
2. Sales performance varied across cities.
3. Customer ratings differed between product categories.
4. Sales trends revealed periods of higher business activity.
